Protect Your Organization Against QR Code Phishing

QR code phishing is an emerging threat where attackers use QR codes to trick users into visiting malicious websites or revealing sensitive information. Microsoft Defender for Office offers protection mechanisms to help organizations secure their employees from these threats.

By leveraging machine learning and advanced threat detection, Microsoft Defender scans emails and attachments containing QR codes, ensuring that malicious links are identified before they can reach the user.

This new feature, aimed at protecting organizations from QR code-based attacks, is part of Microsoft's commitment to enhancing email security.

For more information, visit the official blog post by Microsoft: Protect Your Organization Against QR Code Phishing.
